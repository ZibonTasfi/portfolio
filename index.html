Volunteering Projects

Volunteering Projects

These are a few examples of volunteer work I have done..

Shobuj Bangla

Volunteer for Bangladesh, Kushtia District, organized a project called "Shobuj Bangla" (Green Bengal). The dedicated volunteers, known as the Yellow Warriors, implemented a tree plantation program in various locations, where they planted saplings and raised awareness among the local community about the importance of nurturing the trees. The primary goal of the initiative was to contribute to making the country greener and moving it a step closer to a more sustainable future.

Quality Education

One way to show respect for a language is by using correct spelling and pronunciation. While we're careful with English, mistakes in Bengali often go unquestioned, leading to widespread incorrect usage. To promote better language skills and align with SDG Goal 4 on "Quality Education," Volunteer for Bangladesh, Kushtia District, held a spelling correction competition on September 19th at Lahini High School. The event, organized for students in grades 6-10, awarded valuable educational materials to winners and participants across three groups.

Let's Clean Together

On June 5th, 2024, in celebration of World Environment Day, Volunteer for Bangladesh, Kushtia District, organized a cleanup and awareness program titled "Let's Clean Together." As part of this initiative, volunteers cleaned unused plastic from Kushtia Court Station and surrounding areas, while raising awareness among local shop owners about keeping their premises clean and properly disposing of waste in designated areas. Additionally, waste bins were distributed, and awareness posters were placed in key locations throughout the city to promote environmental responsibility.
